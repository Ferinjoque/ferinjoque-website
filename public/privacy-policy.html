3. Information We Collect

I collect information to understand website usage, improve user experience, ensure security, and respond to inquiries. The types of information collected include:

a) Information Collected via Custom Analytics (Subject to Your Consent):

When you consent to analytics tracking via our cookie banner, the following information may be collected and processed:

  • Event Type: The specific action taken (e.g., `page_view`, `section_view`, `click`, `hover`).
  • Event Details (`payload`): Contextual data associated with the event, stored in a structured format (JSONB). This may include: URL visited, page title, page pathname, section viewed, element interacted with, text content of the element, target URL of clicked links, UTM Parameters (source, medium, campaign, term, content) from the URL if present, and the timestamp of the event.
  • Session ID: A randomly generated unique identifier stored in `sessionStorage` used solely to link together events occurring during a single browsing session. This ID is temporary and is cleared when you close your browser tab/window.
  • IP Address: Your Internet Protocol address. Collected for approximate unique visitor counting and GeoIP lookups (see below). Stored with event logs.
  • User Agent: Information about your browser type, version, and operating system.
  • Referrer URL: The address of the webpage you came from before visiting this site.
  • Device Information: Screen width and height, browser viewport width and height.
  • Browser Information: Preferred language, inferred timezone.

b) Information Collected via Contact Form:

If you use the contact form, I collect: Your Name, Your Email Address, Your Message Content. This information is processed via Formspree.

c) Information Collected for Security (reCAPTCHA):

This website uses Google reCAPTCHA v3 on the contact form to protect against spam. reCAPTCHA collects hardware/software information and sends it to Google for analysis. This operates under Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for:

  • Website Analytics: To understand usage patterns, identify popular content, analyze traffic sources, understand general demographics (browser, device, location), and generate reports to improve the site.
  • AI-Driven Insights: Aggregated, anonymized analytics summaries (not individual logs) may be sent weekly to OpenAI API to generate high-level insights on usage patterns.
  • GeoIP Location: To determine approximate visitor location (country, region, city) using ipinfo.io for aggregated audience analysis.
  • Security: Using Google reCAPTCHA v3 to protect the contact form.
  • Communication: To respond to contact form inquiries.
  • Functionality: To ensure the website works correctly.
